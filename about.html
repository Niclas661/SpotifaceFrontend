Spotiface

Spotiface was made using AngularJS, HTML5 and LESS, with the backend made in C# using the ASP.NET Web API template.

How it all works

Emotion recognition

First, Spotiface needs to get data about your face: you submit a photo or take a picture on your mobile phone, and that image is processed using the Affectiva Emotion SDK.

The SDK returns data about your emotions, but also your expressions like jaw drop or eyes closing. These will be sent to the Spotiface backend for further analysis.

Genre Finding

In the backend, the face recognition data will be used to find your current mood and what genre to suggest. Since data about eyebrow furrow, lip stretching and so forth is taken into consideration when finding a genre, two happy people might not get the same results!
